A comprehensive, interactive Power BI dashboard analyzing the Electric Vehicle (EV) market performance and adoption trends across India.

## üéØ Project Objective

The primary objective of this project is to provide a **comprehensive, data-driven view** of EV adoption trends, market share, and geographical distribution in India. The insights are designed to help stakeholders and policymakers make informed decisions for sustainable mobility growth.

## üí° Problem Statement

With the rapid evolution of the electric vehicle industry in India, stakeholders face challenges in:
*   Understanding **state-wise EV adoption patterns**.
*   Identifying **leading manufacturers** and their market shares.
*   Tracking **yearly growth, sales penetration, and category-wise performance**.
*   Visualizing trends and insights from large, unstructured datasets.

***

*   **Four-wheelers** hold **7.4%** of the sales value (‚Çπ152.94K), showing emerging potential.
